How to Use the App

Step 1: Go to the Upload Page

Click the "Get Started" button on the home page or use the navigation bar to access the image upload section.

Step 2: Upload a Brain MRI Image

Drag and drop or select a valid brain MRI image from your computer. Make sure the image is clear and in JPG or PNG format.

Step 3: Let the AI Analyze

Once uploaded, our AI model will analyze the image and classify it as either healthy or containing a tumor (glioma, meningioma, or pituitary).

Step 4: View Results

The app will show the prediction results along with a Grad-CAM heatmap, highlighting regions of interest used for the decision.

Step 5: Download Report

You can download a PDF report summarizing the result and insights for medical consultation or record keeping.

Need Help?

Check out the About page or reach out to our Developer team for support. Remember, this is a support tool and not a substitute for professional diagnosis.
